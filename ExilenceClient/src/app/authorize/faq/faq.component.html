1: Why does my net worth or inventory not update at all?

Make sure you've entered a valid path to Client.txt when logging in. If it still doesn't work,
try starting the app as administrator. The Client.txt can be found in the same location as the game is installed.
A common mistake is to select the file from an old installation.

 2: I'm getting "The sessionId or accountname is not valid" when trying to login?

The way we verify your sessionId right now is by fetching the first tab in Standard.
If you're certain the accountname is correct (not email), log in on a character in Standard and open the stash,
then log out and try again.

 3: How often does my character update?

This is variable depending on which tab you have selected. Currency-information is updated approximately
once very 2 minutes, but your character and inventory is updated each time you enter a new zone.

 4: My inventory is not displayed at all, only equipment

This is most likely caused from a missing sessionId. Logout/restart the app and re-enter your sessionId.
If the problem persists, contact us on Discord.
