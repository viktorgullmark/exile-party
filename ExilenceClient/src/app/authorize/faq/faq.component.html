Q: My app suddently stopped updating and seems out of sync?

If this happens, it's most likely due to the party being inactive (for 15 minutes), or us updating the API, both of which disconnects you. Restart the app to continue using it. If it keeps happening after restarting, submit a bug-report in our Discord.

 Q: How often does my character update?

This is variable depending on which tab you have selected. Currency-information is updated approximately once very 5 minutes, but your character and inventory is updated each time you enter a new zone.

 Q: Why does my character not update at all?

Make sure you've entered a valid path to Client.txt when logging in. If it still doesn't work, try starting the app as administrator.

 Q: My inventory is not displayed at all, only equipment

This is most likely caused from an invalid or missing sessionId. Logout/restart the app and re-enter your sessionId. If the problem persists, contact us on discord.

 Q: The program suddently stopped tracking area or currency

This is a known issue at the moment. We've been publishing a hotfix for this recently, but if it still occurs as you read this, contact us on Discord and we'll try to help.

 Q: One or more of my partymembers are not updating

If this happens, tell your partymember to restart the app and see if the problem still persists. It should not keep happening with the new reconnect functionality, but if it does, please contact us.
